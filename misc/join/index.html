Join

We are always looking for curiosity-driven and dedicated scientists to join our group.

Our research interests include viruses, viral diseases, viral evolution, genomics, computational biology, and data visualization

Undergraduate Researchers

Freshman, sophomore or junior students typically start in the lab as volunteers. After a 2-3 month period in this capacity, opportunities are generally available for directed research, or paid part-time research. Interested students are encouraged to email a cover letter, resume, and academic transcript (mark.stengleinobfuscate@colostate.edu). Two letters of reference may be requested subsequently.

Graduate students

Graduate students in the MIP and CMB graduate programs at CSU can join the group. Students from other departments may join as well. Rotation students are welcome.

Postdoctoral fellows

Please send (1) cover letter that includes the names and contacts for three references and a short statement of research interests, (2) and a current CV to mark.stengleinobfuscate@colostate.edu

DVM students and post-DVM Residents

Researchers with a clinical perspective are very welcome in the lab. Please inquire about research opportunities.
