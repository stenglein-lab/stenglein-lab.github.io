About this website

Origin

This website is based on that of Trevor Bedford at the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center, who has created a powerful site generation and project collaboration framework that he has made freely available for download.

Logo

The logo combines elements of two of our research interests: viruses and data visualization. The logo is a chord diagram created using d3, a powerful interactive data visualization tool. The shape of the diagram evokes a virus particle.
