Justin Lee

Research Scientist

Justin Lee is a graduate of the DVM/PhD program at Colorado State University. Justin ran the day to day operations of the NGS facility, during which time he was involved in research with the Mayo and Vandewoude labs.

Justin moved on the the CDC in Atlanta in 2019 to lead their NGS core facility.
