Stenglein Lab
Welcome to the Stenglein lab at Colorado State University

We apply a blend of genomic, computational, and traditional laboratory techniques to study human and animal viruses and the diseases they (sometimes) cause.

The lab is just getting underway and we are looking to recruit students, postdocs, and technicians to join in the fun.
